* Business Understanding: Blangkon Cafe is a company engaged in the food and beverage sector. Currently, Blangkon Cafe is growing rapidly and focuses on selling various types of Traditional and Modern food and beverages. Blangkon Cafe will focus on increasing sales innovation so that it can continue to increase sales profit.

* Data Understanding: The dataset used contains the Date and Time, Receipt ID, Product Name, Product Category, Number of Products, and so on.

* Data Preparation: The data used does not include all the columns in the dataset. Some variables that will not use in the filter first. Then the dataset used is a dataset that only contains variables: Product Name, Product Category, Number of Products, and Cashier.

* Modelling: Association Rule is a Data Mining technique to find associative rules between a combination of items with other items. The algorithm used for this association is FP-Growth. FP-Growth is used to find out the purchasing pattern of a consumer's goods.

* Evaluation: The modelling results show that customers who buy Geprek Eggs, Alqodiri Mineral Water, Ginger Milk Drink, Tubruk, and Macaroni Cio, also tend to buy Wedang Uwuh with a Confidence (conf) value = 1. If the Confidence (conf) value is close to 1 or even equal to 1, the modelling results are getting better.

* Deployment: Based on the analysis results, we as Data Scientists try to offer solutions to company owners for Deployment or implementation. Our Solutions is Offering Wedang Uwuh products to buyers when buying Geprek Eggs, Alqodiri Mineral Water, Ginger Milk Drink, Tubruk, and Macaroni Cio.
